🔕 Block Notifications During Focus

Your phone can't do this from a webpage — but here's the exact setup:

📱
iPhone / iOS
  1. Settings → Focus → Do Not Disturb
  2. Tap "Add Schedule" → set your focus hours
  3. Allow only essential contacts
  4. Enable Focus Filters to hide distracting apps
🤖
Android
  1. Settings → Digital Wellbeing → Focus Mode
  2. Select apps to pause during focus
  3. Settings → Sound → Do Not Disturb
  4. Schedule automatic DND during work hours

🆘 When the Urge is Strong — Do One of These NOW

🏃
Move Your Body
20 jumping jacks, a brisk 5-min walk, or 10 push-ups. Physical movement redirects the dopamine spike.
📞
Call Someone
Reach out to a friend, family member, or accountability partner. Connection beats the screen every time.
⏱️
Surf the Urge (10 min)
Urges peak at ~10 min then fade. Set a timer. Breathe. The wave will pass.
🚿
Cold Shower
Splash cold water on your face or take a brief cold shower. Immediately resets your arousal system.
📖
Read Your Why
Go to Commitments and re-read why you started this journey. Reconnect with your values.
🙏
Pray / Meditate
Even 3 minutes of prayer, gratitude, or silent breathing can shift the mental state entirely.
